Optimal pandemic mitigation

Interactive demonstrator. Drag the blue handles, slide the cost exponents, watch the trade-off.

A simple SIRS epidemic with adjustable mitigation. The infection cost is CI = Iα, the mitigation cost is CM = M / (1 − Mζ), and the severity a sets their relative weight. The paper proves that for α ≤ 1 and ζ ≥ 1 the optimal mitigation is exactly all-or-nothing: M* ∈ {0, 1 − 1/R0}.
